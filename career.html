Careers

We, at Future Savings Co-operative Limited strongly believe that a high-quality human capital makes the difference and add value in achieving our vision of being the best out of all. We are always on a lookout for competent, result oriented and self-motivated people who can deliver the result and are eager to grow along with us.

If you are eager to be part of the future team that strives to excel in whatever it does, you may send your curriculum vitae to futuresavecoperative.com.np. All applications and curriculum vitae will be stored in our Human Resource database.

Should there be any suitable vacancy, we will review the database and contact you as and when required.

Thank you.
